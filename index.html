BIG GUS D VARIETIES

Since the dawn of time all human beings have clashed with each other, whether for the possession of territories or resources, with the sole aim of satisfying their insatiable egos and proving their superiorities.

Following events still unknown, it seems that the human ego of all these people have come to life, giving birth to a new intelligent species with a questionable aspect: the Big Gus D

Much like the human beings from whom the egos originated in their creations, it seems that these creatures have many different characteristics, including thickness and length. Our teams counted more than 100 !
They also seem to have inherited the same motivation to prove their superiority from their creator, since their only goal is to determine who has the biggest .

In order to avoid the disasters that could result from an internal war on these unknown creatures, our team decided to seek by other means the answer to their question.
Join us in this quest to find Who Has The Biggest, and save humanity.
